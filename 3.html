Trek

Ryan Howard is one of the smoothest, most stylish riders to swing a leg over a mountain bike. The Santa Cruz-area local honed his flow skills racing Sandhill Dual slaloms as a grom and is an integral part of the original Post Office jumps crew. He has been sculpting and crafting trails for as long as he's been getting his bike in the sky. Dan Severson caught up with Ryan to check out his current quiver of Trek bikes, including a Rail e-bike. R-Dog has been with Trek for over 10 years now, and shredding with the C3 Project, he has some unique bikes to investigate.
